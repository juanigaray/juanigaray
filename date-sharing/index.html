How to use this page-app

Ever want to share a date with someone else who's in a different timezone? Oh, let's meet friday at, uh, 10 PM GMT! That is very annoying.

There are tools to solve this out there but they tend to do too many things and they're uglier than this website.

This page does one thing. It asks for a date in YOUR timezone and gives you a URL that you can send to people. When someone enters that URL, this page will detect where their browser is and show them the date converted to that timezone.
