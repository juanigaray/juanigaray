Das Triadische Ballet I:

Gelb

This is a small one-sitting excercise I made on a rainy Sunday afternoon. Bauhaus art lends itself very well to recreation in CSS with its simple shapes and colors.

I could not reproduce the angle of the shot and the width of the grid lines with 100% accuracy, as aliasing made the lines disappear erratically, but I had fun trying to reproduce the shadows and imperfections of the film with background gradients which I feel give the image a little depth.

You can watch the video from which I got this frame here, and if you're into electronic music, you can listen to Patricia Taxxon's triple album inspired by this ballet starting here.
